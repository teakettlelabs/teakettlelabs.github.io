Team Bios

Shannon Ladymon - Founder

Shannon has a background in Computational Linguistics (M.S., University of Washington, Seattle) and education, with a previous career in teaching English as a Second Language that was grounded in her desire for social good and has fed into her approach to NLP. She has worked on the management team of a computer science education startup, on a DARPA-funded low-resource language Machine Translation project, and as an Innovation Fellow with the NYC Mayor's Office of the Chief Technology Officer to build an open-source Machine Translation pipeline. Shannon brings expertise in linguistics and NLP and a passion for service.

Sushanth Sowmyan - Founder

Sushanth has a background in Computer Science (M.S., University of Chicago, Chicago) and has worked on Big Data platforms and distributed systems for over 15 years as a data warehouse backend engineer. He was one of the first engineers in Hortonworks, where he made significant open-source contributions to Apache Hive, the petabyte-scale data warehousing solution on Hadoop. More recently, he was the architect for the Data Engineering group at Zillow, where he was responsible for technical roadmaps, getting buy-in from executives for initiatives, alignment between the AI and Data groups, revamping the data architecture, and overseeing the technical work of about 60 people. Sushanth brings experience in Big Data, open source ecosystems, and the enterprise space.

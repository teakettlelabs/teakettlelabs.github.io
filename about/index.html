Core Principles

We believe in a set of core principles that guide our interactions, decisions, and operations.

Cultivate Change
Be intentional, sustainable, and make things better.
  • Expect change and develop a growth mindset.
  • Leave the place better than you found it.
  • Think sustainability and prefer doing fewer things well.
Bird's-eye View
Take a long-term view that focuses on systemic rather than individual actions.
  • Prioritize long-term impact over short-term gain.
  • Changing systems will have a larger and wider impact.
  • Consider secondary and systemic effects of all actions.
Center the Margin
Start with inclusivity and equity for all as a foundation.
  • Solving for the margins serves the center as well.
  • Design with a focus on access and outcomes.
  • Be mindful of which viewpoints are not able to be in the room.
We're in This Together
Create the best shared outcomes through kindness, listening and consensus.
  • Be kind and empathetic to others and yourself.
  • Respect individual, group and cultural dignity.
  • Consensus takes time but leads to better outcomes.
